Information On Ocean Pollution


Hover over the "steps" to see some ways to stop ocean pollution!



Plastic doesn't belong in the ocean, recycle to save ocean life.




Ocean pollution is a growing threat to our planet. As our oceans cover about 71% of the Earth's surface, however human activities have resulted in the accumulation of various pollutants in our oceans. Things contributing to ocean pollution is plastics, chemicals, and oil spills. These pollutants have a devastating effect on marine ecosystem, wildlife, and even human health, this is why we need to start recycling and save the ocean.
