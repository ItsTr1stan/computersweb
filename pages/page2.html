4 Types Of Waste Management!



A community can produce four main types of waste: plant waste, commercial waste, domestic waste, and industrial waste. These can be narrowed down to waste produced in farms, commercial establishments, offices, schools, households, factories, and manufacturing industries.

Waste disposal, use designated trash cans and recycling bins, and avoid littering. Dispose of hazardous materials, such as batteries and chemicals, at appropriate collection centers to prevent them from entering the ocean.
