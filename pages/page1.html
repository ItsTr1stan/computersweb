Recycling in your community!



The first step to make a difference is to go to your local return it depot and ask what bottles they take. Some examples are glass bottles, juice containers, soup cans, liquor plastic, and liqour glass. US liquor bottles are not accepted at the return it depot.

Link for some examples from the return it depot:

https://www.return-it.ca/beverage/recycling/onedeposit/


Link for Port Coquitlam return it depot:


https://www.return-it.ca/locations/coquitlam-return-it-depot

Remember that there are more things to recycle than just plastic bottles, there is paper recycling, glass, metal, electronic waste, battery, organic waste and more.
